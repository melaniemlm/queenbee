Introduction To Bee-Keeping

This one-day course is designed as an introduction for potential new beekeepers or beekeepers with less than 2 years experience.

The morning is a classroom lecture and the afternoon is a practical demonstration within our hives to allow you "hands on" experience.

We will cover all aspects of what you should be looking for at your hive inspection so that you can understand the life cycle and learn to "read" your bees.

Protective clothing will be supplied if required but please bring stout boots or "wellies". Lunch will be provided.

Course will run 10am till 4pm

£30.00 per person
