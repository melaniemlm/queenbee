About Queen Bee

We are an independent family-run café and shop where we sell our handmade organic products that use only the finest locally sourced ingredients.

The honey in all of our products is provided by local honey bees direct from our hives. We also offer beekeeping courses that are suitable for beginners to the avid backyard beekeeper.

Bee Keeping

We offer a one day course to introduce you to the art of beekeeping and care of our little busy friends.
